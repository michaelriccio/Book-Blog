Title: Made In Abyss

Author: Akihito Tsukushi
Genre: Fantasy
Uploaded: Feb 25, 2020

When explorers find a massive hole bored miles into the Earth, they find mystic treasures, and monsters. They try to return to the surface, though find themself under the Curse of the Abyss. Returning upwards pushes the body to its limits, causing nasuea and dizziness near the surface, and internal bleeding and hallucinations near the center. Soon rising mere feet will causes instand death. One orphan girl seeks to reach the bottom. What is she willing to sacrifice?
