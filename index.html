Title: Moby Dick

Author: Herman Melville
Genre: Adventure
Uploaded: In-Progress

Moby Dick tells the story of a rover named Ishmael, who takes to the seas when land-life becomes too depressing a task. Our protagonist sets his sights on greater achievements than a mere ship hand, he seeks to work a whaling vessle. As Ishmael makes his way to the Whaling Capital "Nantucket" he meets a strange man. Queequeg, as he calls himself, hails from Africa and left his life as a prince to explore the Christian world. Together they set off on a once in a lifetime voyage.

Title: Stand Still Stay Silent

Author: Minna Sundberg
Genre: Adventure
Uploaded: April 15, 2020

Humanity was unprepared when a mutagenic virus swept the world, obliterating cites and the people within. Society may have recovered if the virus hadn't had it's second effect. Reanimation. What dies in this world comes back as a grotesque impersonation of an organism, as the newly dead sprout limbs and other gangrious fleshy additions. For the past hundred years the inhabitants of this new world have fought back to reclaim Scandinavia, though the monsters that lurk in the darkness can never truly be defeated.

Title: Watership Down

Author: Richard Adams
Genre: Fantasy
Uploaded: Mar 28 2020

When a Prophet forsees great destruction for their warren, a band of Rabbits venture out into the unknown to build a new home. Though preadators are never kind to the small and fragile. Soon the group is beset by beasts of the night, wandering humans, and worst of all, their own kind. Is freedom worth the risk? What will the rabbits have to do to build and defend a new warren? And who is the Rabbit that calls your name in the middle of the Night.

Title: On Writing

Author: Stephen King
Genre: Self-Improvement
Uploaded: Mar 11, 2020

In this Novel, writer Stephen King elaborates on his past, and the lessons that he learned on his way to becoming a writer. Though don't think that this book is a mere memoir. Stephen breaks down his craft with the tact and charm that you would expect from one of Americas greatest authors. If you're interested in writing as a hobby or career, this book is a must have.

Title: Made In Abyss

Author: Akihito Tsukushi
Genre: Fantasy
Uploaded: Feb 25, 2020

When explorers find a massive hole bored miles into the Earth, they find mystic treasures, and monsters. They try to return to the surface, though find themself under the Curse of the Abyss. Returning upwards pushes the body to its limits, causing nasuea and dizziness near the surface, and internal bleeding and hallucinations near the center. Soon rising mere feet will causes instand death. One orphan girl seeks to reach the bottom. What is she willing to sacrifice?
